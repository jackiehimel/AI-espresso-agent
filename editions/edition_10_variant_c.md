## China is training home robots by filming people fold laundry

Chinese AI companies are collecting robotics training data directly in homes and small factories, recording ordinary people doing household tasks like folding clothes and washing dishes. The approach costs less and scales faster than the U.S. model of lab research and outsourced annotation, giving China an edge in teaching robots to handle real-world messiness.

*Whoever collects the best mundane-task data first will likely dominate the home robotics market.*
