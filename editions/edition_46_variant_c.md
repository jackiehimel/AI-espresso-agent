## Apple can finally ship Apple Intelligence to China

Apple reached an agreement with Beijing to bring Siri and other AI features to Chinese iPhones by partnering with local AI companies. Chinese law requires foreign tech companies to use domestic AI models, which blocked Apple Intelligence's rollout there since last year's iOS 27 beta launch.

*China is Apple's third-largest market—this deal unlocks AI features for hundreds of millions of iPhones.*

[9to5Mac — AI](https://9to5mac.com/2026/07/15/apple-reaches-agreement-with-chinese-government-on-apple-intelligence-rollout/) · Jul 15
