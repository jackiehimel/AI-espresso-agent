## Microsoft shut down 70 of its own repos after hackers pushed malware targeting AI coding agents

Microsoft closed more than 70 GitHub repositories it owned after attackers compromised them to distribute credential-stealing malware. *Supply chain attacks now explicitly target AI agents, not just human developers.*
